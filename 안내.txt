공공데이터와 AI챗봇을 이용한 물가 음성안내 앱 서비스
Audio Guidance Application For Commodity Prices Using Public Data And AI Chatbot

Abstract

The recent price instability has caused price fluctuations in agricultural and fisheries products to
increase, and consumers buy products based on market marketing or experience and sense without
exact standards. Therefore, it is a natural language application of AI chetbot and voice recognition
function that can inform consumers of price index of agricultural products in real time using public data.
